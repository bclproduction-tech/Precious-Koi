Design a premium, modern, corporate portfolio website for an IT Technician and Sales Representative. The objective of the website is to establish credibility, showcase professional experience, and encourage companies, schools, hotels, hospitals, and organizations to partner with her for their IT equipment and technology needs.

The overall feeling should be clean, elegant, trustworthy, premium, minimal, and corporate, comparable to the quality of websites from Microsoft, Dell, Lenovo, HP, or Apple—not flashy, but refined and highly professional.

Visual Style

Every section should feel premium enough that a hotel, university, hospital, or corporate office immediately trusts the professionalism.

Use generous whitespace.

Avoid clutter.

Keep every section aligned to a centered container.

Maintain consistent spacing using an 8px design system.

Use subtle geometric decorative burgundy shapes sparingly.

The final result should look like it was designed by an award-winning UI/UX agency and be suitable for converting corporate visitors into potential clients.

One additional recommendation

Since this portfolio is meant to win high-end clients, I would include one section that is truthful but significantly increases trust:

Why Choose Me

This section should not invent any claims, but instead present the existing information in a polished way, for example:

Qualified in IT, Accounting (QuickBooks), and Graphic Design
Technician & Sales Representative at Josmer Computers
Experience working with organizations across Mombasa
Computer equipment and accessories supplier
Installation and maintenance services

These are all based on the information you provided, but presenting them as concise trust points makes the website feel more persuasive without adding anything that isn't true. This small addition can have a significant impact on how professional the portfolio appears.
